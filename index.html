About Us

China's Trusted Fastener
Manufacturer & Exporter

Founded in 2005, FastenersGlobal Industrial Co., Ltd. is a leading manufacturer and global exporter of industrial fasteners and hardware components. Our state-of-the-art factory produces millions of precision parts each year.

We specialise in gaskets, wood screws, high-strength bolts, shackles, and clamps, serving clients across Africa, the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia. All products are manufactured to international standards including ISO, DIN, ASME, and BS.
